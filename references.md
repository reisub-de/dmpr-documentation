# References

In this document we list the references used in the creation of dmpr. The goal was to compile good ideas and to avoid the biggest problems in other protocols.

## OLSRv1
* [RFC 3626](https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc3626)
* ["Optimized Link State Routing Protocolfor Ad Hoc Networks"](http://edge.cs.drexel.edu/regli/Classes/CS680/Papers/OLSR-gwpark_040618.ppt)

## Babel Routing Protocol
* [RFC 6216](https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc6126)
* ["Everything you wanted to know about the Babel routing protocol but were afraid to ask" by Juliusz Chroboczek](http://battlemesh.org/BattleMeshV8/Agenda?action=AttachFile&do=get&target=babel-20150804.pdf)

## BGPv4
* [RFC 4271](https://tools.ietf.org/html/rfc4271)
